Roachella grew up being mocked and rejected for being a banana roach. Constantly “yucked” by others who associate roaches with grime she developed an obsession with cleanliness. Determined to prove she belongs she took work as a maid and became a compulsive cleaner. However, despite Roachella’s dedication ... she’s wildly clueless about how the human world works. In her mind breaking into someone’s house isn’t a crime… it’s just “helping them tidy up.” She may genuinely believe she’s doing a good deed even while committing complete chaos.
