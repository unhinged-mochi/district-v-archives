Agua Fresca is a fresh-faced young hopeful from a small lil underwater town of Atlantis. An ex-merman freshly turned human, he's not used to using his human legs, or his arms, or... any of his non-fishy parts. He tries his best to be as cool and normal as possible, but humans are hard to understand sometimes.. maybe it's culture shock? He's clumsy, is afraid of cats and sometimes talks to the sea life. His dreams are pretty mundane; make friends, find a good job, conquer his biggest fears, and live amongst the people! But can a little fish guy from a little fish town really make it in the big city?
