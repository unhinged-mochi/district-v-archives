Born in a wealthy family with a line of police chiefs on her father's side and lawyers and doctors on her mother's side, it seemed like she had it all. Yet despite her father enacting justice in the public eye, the hands of justice would never reach within the four walls of home. The abuse from her father drove her mother to madness, alcohol addiction, before running away with another man and dying of liver failure. As the abuse worsens in the absence of her mother, Roku Nana and her father sought to kill each other one day in an argument, when a criminal shootout tangles them up. The weakened Roku faints before identifying the mysterious criminal who kills her abusive father. A hollow, empty orphan now set ablaze by her anger and desire to enact true justice in the occupation she once swore to never join. Rumour has it that the criminal she seeks is now in a gang in Los Santos. Will she be able to find him?
