Abandoned as a child by her parents and unable to find a foster family that could deal with her antics and horrible attitude, Anya never came to understand what love truly was. Family was nothing to her, relationships meant nothing to her, and as a result, she continuously turned to a life of crime, only being able to feel a serotonin boost from making others feel a similar pain that she was forced to feel all her life. After Anya's 13th failed relationship and crime ring exposure in her previous city, she decided to move to a new city and start her life over (again) and do things the right way this time -- living a normal life without needing to turn to a life of crime... but will the luxuries and treasures of her new city tempt her into the only life she has known...?
