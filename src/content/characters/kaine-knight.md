Earl Greywolfe is the owner of A-woooo-long Tea, a boba shop in another city. When Earl heard his long-lost twin was spotted in Los Santos, he abandoned his boba shop to chase the ghost of a sibling he thought was dead. Separated at birth by a catastrophic hospital fire that claimed their parents' lives, Earl’s only link to his past is a charred, obscured photograph. He holds only one half; haunted by the hope that a sibling he’s never met might still hold the other. Driven by a compulsive need to do the right thing, Earl often finds himself in over his head, trading the safety of the counter for the chaos of the streets. The search begins now. He’ll find them... No matter what.
