A former F1 driver who used to compete in the Grand Prix. After becoming the number 2 driver in his team, he quit racing and now he is struggling to live the days without the thrill of the competition. He seeks the adrenaline, the excitement, and most importantly, the dangers this world has to offer. To him, the definition of living is to be able to get his blood pumping on the road at extremely high speeds. He sits back home, far from the city and asks himself the same question every day: “Have I truly lived the life I wanted?”
