Grew up in a quiet coastal town, singing for years in small cafés and local venues. People complimented his voice, enjoyed the ambiance, and then returned to their conversations. No matter how much passion he poured into his performances, he always felt like background music in someone else’s story. Over time, that quiet indifference weighed on him. He realized he didn’t just want to be heard — he wanted to be felt. So he embraced something bigger: a more dramatic persona, a sharper presence, a version of himself that refused to fade into the background. He moved to Los Santos chasing adventure, emotion, and the kind of attention that turns a singer into something unforgettable.
