Singer by day, assassin by night. Shun Nightsong was born musically gifted and developed his talents since a young age. However, the assassination of his parents by jealous rivals angered him so much that he trained to be one himself in order to take revenge. After enacting his revenge, he was scouted by Cosmix, an organization that functions as a music agency on the outside but in secret sends out their talents to enact justice on wanted criminals.
