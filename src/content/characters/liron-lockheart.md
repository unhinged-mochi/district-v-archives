After a tragic past and a devastating fight, he lost four of the most precious members of his found family. Somehow, he survived. But at the cost of his memory. Calling himself “Lucky,” he developed an obsession with gambling, treating every choice in life as a wager. From then on, all his important decisions were determined by a simple 50/50 coin flip. He will live life to its fullest, no matter the outcome. Will he turn out good or bad? Perhaps there’s only a 50/50 chance.
