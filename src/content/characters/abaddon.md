A failed magician? A flirty host? A slight alcoholic? Damon is all three! Damon came to the city hoping to mend his fractured magic, long silent and unresponsive. As sorrow led him to the bottle and his coin ran dry, he abandoned spellcraft almost entirely, relying only on his charm and good looks to survive as a host.
