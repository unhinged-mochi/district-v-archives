Born and raised in Ballet Town, sharing a love for dance with her mother and a love for games with her father who chose her name inspired by the protagonist of his favorite game series, Ashe Nee-chan was a very happy child... That was until she grew up and her name started costing her professional opportunities and serious relationships with her peers. Tired of this, Ashe decided to leave both dance and games behind, and she moved towns to start anew with a different name, far away from the pain those experiences had caused her. However, when she arrived to Los Santos she felt a strange force taking over her... A ghost of her past controlling her mind and behavior, Will she defeat this ghost and find herself? Only time will tell! But something urges her to catch them all, whether it be professionally as a police officer or independently as a vigilante is yet to be known.. One thing's for certain: She's... GOTTA CATCH EM ALL.
