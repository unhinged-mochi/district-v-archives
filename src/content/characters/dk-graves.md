Moral Compass: Chaotic Evil. Mr Toad was cancelled after swallowing an innocent cameraman on his show and refusing to apologize. In an attempt to salvage his image, the Smiling Pals assigned their most optimistic employee, Bim, to help Mr Toad “learn empathy” and reintegrate into society. The relocation to the city was framed as a fresh start and a rebrand. Mr Toad’s ultimate goal is to reclaim his fame, restore his public image, and maybe.. just maybe commit just a little bit of crime along the way.
