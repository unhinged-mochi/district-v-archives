Mei is just a tired old woman who was the wife of Mafia Boss who has passed away. She has a deep smoker voice from all the packs she has had over the years. In her younger years she was known to organize many heists for her husband. Now she is a widow, a tired mob wife, with lots of money to travel around. Even though she won't admit it, she has a couple of screws loose and can't recall everything she does due to her old age. She may come off as unfriendly at first but that is just because being too friendly could lead to betrayal, a lesson learned from her younger years. All she looks forward to nowadays is the simple luxuries life has to offer in her older years.
