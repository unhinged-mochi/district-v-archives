## -- Aura's Third Store Robbery
[Louise Cielo](dossier:derem-kado), [Mr. Toad](dossier:dk-graves), and [Anita Heist](dossier:byeolyuna_) attempted a third store robbery, taking Agua Fresca as a hostage. The police chase ended in capture.

## -- Aura's Fourth Store Robbery — The Scuba Incident
Aura's most successful heist to date. [Louis Cielo](dossier:rai-galilei), [Mr. Toad](dossier:dk-graves), and [River Kawa](dossier:ryu-hari) took two hostages — Toro Taunano and Bim Bimling — and executed what is now referred to as the **Scuba Tactics** escape: flooding two police vehicles, drowning two officers, both hostages, and one civilian. The crew escaped.

## -- Gang Aura Officially Formed
Following the day's operations, [Louis Cielo](dossier:rai-galilei) — operating under the alias **Crow** — formally established Gang Aura with five founding members: [Mr. Toad](dossier:dk-graves), [Alysse Itried](dossier:hachi), [River Kawa](dossier:ryu-hari), [Anita Heist](dossier:byeolyuna_), and [Jennifer](dossier:jelly).
