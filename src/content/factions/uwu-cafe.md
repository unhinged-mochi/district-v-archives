A cat-themed cafe in Los Santos owned by [NekoRiko](dossier:riko-solari), serving as a social hub and cover for some questionable activities. Several El Batchelors members — [Luca Santos](dossier:luca-kaneshiro), [Shu Yamino](dossier:shu-yamino), and [Jimmy Umami](dossier:sanpaku) — work here as cover. [Tim](dossier:jurard-rexford) of BGC also works here.
