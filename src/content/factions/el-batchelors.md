Known as the "Animal Mask Mafia." Started as small-time crooks who couldn't rob $3 from a hobo. Became the city's most dangerous gang in under a week. Wear animal masks and white suits during operations. Led by [Luca Santos](dossier:luca-kaneshiro), with [Shu Yamino](dossier:shu-yamino), [Jimmy Umami](dossier:sanpaku), [Sonny Briscoops](dossier:sonny-brisko), and [Yukyun Willerton](dossier:yu-q-wilson). Several members use the uwu Cafe as cover.

## Operations
- Bank robberies (Fleeca Bank chain)
- Armed robbery
- Grand theft auto
- Territory control

## Notable Events
- Day 1: Failed $3 hobo robbery (humble beginnings)
- Day 3: First successful Fleeca Bank heist
- Day 7: Most members arrested and sent to prison
- Ongoing confrontations with LSPD
